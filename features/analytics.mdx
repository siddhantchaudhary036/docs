ReleaseDock tracks how your users interact with your changelogs across all channels.

## View tracking

Every time a user views a changelog — whether on the public page or through the widget — it's recorded. Views are deduplicated by fingerprint so repeat visits from the same user don't inflate your numbers.

Each view tracks:
- Which changelog was viewed
- The source (widget or public page)
- Timestamp

## Reactions

Users can react to changelogs with emoji reactions on the public changelog page. Reactions are tracked per changelog and deduplicated by fingerprint.

## Email analytics

When email notifications are enabled, ReleaseDock tracks email engagement events:

| Event | Description |
|---|---|
| Sent | Email was sent to the subscriber |
| Delivered | Email was delivered to the inbox |
| Opened | Subscriber opened the email |
| Clicked | Subscriber clicked a link in the email |
| Bounced | Email bounced (hard or soft) |

View email analytics per changelog from the dashboard.

## Dashboard

The analytics dashboard gives you an overview of your project's performance, including total views, reaction counts, and email engagement metrics. Navigate to **Dashboard > Analytics** to see the full picture.
