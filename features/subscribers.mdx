ReleaseDock lets users subscribe to your changelog and receive email notifications whenever you publish a new entry.

## How users subscribe

Subscribers can sign up through:

- The embeddable widget (if the subscribe form is enabled)
- Your public changelog page
- Manual addition from the dashboard
- CSV import

Each subscriber gets a unique preference token for managing their subscription.

## Email notifications

When you publish a changelog, ReleaseDock can automatically send an email to all active subscribers. Emails are sent via Resend.

You can enable or disable auto-notify in **Settings > Email**.

## Email customization

Customize the look of your notification emails in **Settings > Email**:

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| Sender name | The "from" name on the email |
| Sender email | The "from" address |
| Logo URL | Logo displayed in the email header |
| Button color | Color of the CTA button |
| Link color | Color of links in the email body |
| Background color | Email background |
| Text color | Email body text color |

## Subscriber management

From **Settings > Subscribers**, you can:

- View all subscribers with their status and source
- See subscriber statuses: active, unsubscribed, bounced, or complained
- Track which channel each subscriber came from (widget, public page, manual, CSV import)

## Unsubscribe and preferences

Every email includes an unsubscribe link. Subscribers can also manage their category preferences through a dedicated preferences page, choosing which types of updates they want to receive.
