## Step 1: Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [ReleaseDock](https://app.releasedock.co) and create an account. Authentication is handled through Clerk — you can sign up with email or a social provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    After signing up, you'll be guided through onboarding where you create your first project. Pick a project name, slug, and website URL.

    Your slug determines your public changelog URL: `app.releasedock.co/changelog/your-slug`
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Write your first changelog

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the editor">
    From the dashboard, navigate to **Changelogs** and click **New Changelog**. The block-based editor supports headings, lists, images, and inline formatting via slash commands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add labels">
    Tag your changelog with labels like Feature, Bugfix, or Improvement. Labels are color-coded so readers can scan updates at a glance. You can create custom labels in **Settings > Labels**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish or save as draft">
    When you're ready, click **Publish**. You can also save as a draft and come back later, or schedule it for a future date and time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Embed the widget

Add the ReleaseDock widget to your site with a single script tag:

```html
<script
  src="https://app.releasedock.co/widget.js"
  data-project="YOUR_API_KEY"
  async
></script>
```

<Tip>
  Find your API key in **Settings > Widget** on the dashboard. The widget uses Shadow DOM isolation, so it won't conflict with your site's styles.
</Tip>
