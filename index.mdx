ReleaseDock is a changelog management platform that lets you write, publish, and embed changelogs in minutes. It includes a rich text editor, an embeddable widget, subscriber email notifications, in-app surveys, and a public changelog page.

## Why ReleaseDock?

Most teams ship updates but never tell their users. ReleaseDock closes that gap with a simple workflow: write a changelog, hit publish, and your users see it everywhere — on your public page, inside your app via the widget, and in their inbox.

## Core features

- Rich block-based editor with slash commands, images, and drag-and-drop
- Draft, scheduled, and published workflow
- Embeddable widget with Shadow DOM isolation (zero style conflicts)
- Public changelog page with a dedicated URL per project
- Color-coded labels and categories
- Subscriber management with email notifications via Resend
- In-app surveys (NPS, CSAT, CES, PMF, and custom questions)
- View and reaction analytics
- Multi-project support

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Sign up, pick a name and slug. Your public changelog page is live instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write and publish">
    Use the rich editor to write your update. Add labels, save as draft, or publish immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Embed the widget">
    Copy the one-line script tag into your site. Users see updates the moment you publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>
